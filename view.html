    VISITING TIPS

  • To wear a mask.

  • To have one's temperature taken.

  • Smoking is prohibited anywhere in the museum.

  • Food, beverages, and pets are prohibited.

  • Please refrain from speaking loudly and running in the exhibition halls.

  • Refrain from touching any of the artifacts and exhibits or engage in behavior that might damage them.

  • Photography or videotaping for commercial purposes requires advanced written permission from the museum.

  • Wheeled shoes are prohibited inside the museum.

  • Please wear appropriate attire. Refrain from wearing slippers.
