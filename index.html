ABOUT US

Muzium Negara, located in Kuala Lumpur, is the national museum of Malaysia showcasing the country's cultural and historical heritage. The museum features a diverse collection of artifacts, interactive exhibits, and cutting-edge technology, offering visitors a comprehensive look into Malaysia's past and present. A visit to Muzium Negara provides an enriching experience for anyone interested in learning about the country's rich cultural heritage and its journey from a colonial past to a modern nation.
